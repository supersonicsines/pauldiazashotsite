## Replacement Content

The rebrand wasn't a marketing exercise; it was a chain migration from BSC
(Gambit) to Arbitrum and Avalanche (GMX), with the partner network lined up to
launch alongside.

Gambit was initially successful on BSC, reaching nine-figure TVL and issuing
GUSDT, a stablecoin backed by aggregate long/short position float and funding
fees. The product was thoughtful, but market volatility broke the peg, so the
migration demanded a technical rethink rather than a cosmetic rename.

My role as de facto community lead was to move users into a new product on new
chains without fracturing the community after losses in the first system. It
worked: users who held through the migration saw 500%+ ROI, and the Telegram
community largely stayed intact through the transition.
