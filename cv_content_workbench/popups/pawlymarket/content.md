## Replacement Content

Live scraper and statistical-arbitrage layer over prediction markets. Reads
order books across Kalshi, Polymarket, and smaller venues; identifies systematic
mispricings; surfaces them as actionable calls.

The system scrapes public market data, screens for correlations with Pearson
coefficients, then runs a second pass over divergence, beta, and theta-decay
opportunities. Subscribers can assemble hedged strategies around mean reversion
or geopolitical event risk; a community layer lets users share and discuss
trades.

Built end-to-end with Claude Code and agentic curation at every stage, with
Haiku, Sonnet, and Opus each assigned to the parts of the workflow where they
are strongest.
